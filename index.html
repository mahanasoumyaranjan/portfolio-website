Details

Technical Manager :- Responsible for overseeing technical projects, providing guidance and leadership to the development team, and ensuring the successful delivery of projects within budget and timeline constraints.

R&D Manager :- Leads research and development efforts, coordinates with various teams to develop innovative solutions, and manages the research process from conception to implementation.

Software Engineer (Full-Stack) :- Designs, develops, and maintains both front-end and back-end components of web applications, ensuring high performance, responsiveness, and scalability. Technical Lead: Leads a team of software engineers, provides technical guidance and mentorship, and collaborates with stakeholders to define project requirements and goals.

Software Engineer (Testing) :- Develops and executes test cases, identifies and reports bugs, and works closely with the development team to ensure the quality and reliability of software products.
